INTERJECTION

An Interjection is a word which expresses some sudden feeling or emootion.

Bravo, what a beautiful house!
    Types of interjection:-
  1. Primary Interjections
  2. Secondary Interjections

Primary Interjections:-

This type of interjection refers to the terms that are entirely interjections and cannot be categorized as any other Part of Speech.

  1. Oops, I missed the opportunity.
  2. Wow! This is our golden opportunity to participate in the contest.

Secondary Interjections:-

Secondary Interjections are nouns, adjectives, and other parts of speech that behave as interjections at certain times.

  1. Indeed, I was looking forward to your forewarnings.
  2. Goodness! How did you complete all the tasks in 2 hours.
